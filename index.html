About

Ignite is an evening of fast paced talks on a wide range of interesting subjects, first held in Seattle in 2006. The first event was organized by Brady Forrest and Bre Pettis, inspired in part by a desire to simplify Pecha Kucha to a 5 minute format.

There have been 26 Ignite Seattle events and they are currently held at the wonderful Town Hall Seattle. Many other cities also run Ignite events.
